NFT

NFT artists haven’t been shy about their support for charitable causes or key social issues. An increasing number of NFT projects have taken the initiative to donate a portion of their profits from sales to charities. The selling price of some NFTs reaches into the millions, so we’re not talking about small change.
NFTs are becoming a major force in charitable fundraising. Several projects are donating hundreds of thousands, even millions, of dollars to nonprofit organizations. These generous creators are fostering impactful relationships with charities, both by endowing them with charitable support and by sharing their passion with a devoted fanbase.
